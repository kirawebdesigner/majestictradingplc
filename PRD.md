# Majestic Trading PLC Homepage Concept PRD

## Problem
Majestic Trading PLC has a broad service offering across interiors, finishing, construction, branding, website design, and related business solutions, but the current presentation does not fully express the premium, multidisciplinary nature of the company.

## Audience
- Business owners and operators in Ethiopia seeking fit-out, branding, and digital execution.
- Homeowners and real estate developers seeking residential design, finishing, woodworking, and construction support.
- Hiring reviewers evaluating the quality of a custom homepage concept.

## MVP Scope
- A polished single-page homepage concept.
- Premium visual treatment inspired by the Strux Framer template.
- Rewritten Majestic-specific positioning, service cards, trust sections, process, project placeholders, and contact CTA.
- Responsive desktop, tablet, and mobile experience.

- Project names and some metrics are placeholders for concept presentation.
